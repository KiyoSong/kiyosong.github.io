About

I was born and raised in Hangzhou, a southeast city in China with a lot of rain and green parks. The city is quite close to Shanghai and has been developing fast as we have the famous attraction West Lake and internet business. My journey into photography commenced in 2017, capturing people in the alleys and bustling urban streets. Through my lens, I aimed to document the transformative journey of our city during the period of rapid growth.

Currently nestled in London. The diversity of this city has significantly influenced my photographic style, infusing it with a unique vibrancy as I revel in capturing its distinct lifestyles.

In my view, photography acts as a bridge to connect our lives and the surrounding environment—bridging architecture, streets, societies, individuals, and diversed lifestyle into narrative stories.
